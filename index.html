Use Cases for drones

Use Cases 1: Hovering

Hovering is important due to the main use of frones which is taking video. Being able to stabalize and stay still in an area greatly helps video.
Steps to hovering
1.Get drone up in air
2.Get drone to stay in one x,y,and z axis.

Rotating

Rotating helps move a drone around and makes it more versitale.
Steps to Rotating
1.Get drone up in air
2.Get drone to understand x,y,z axis.
3.Get drone to understand -x,-y,-z axis.
4.set up perameters for responsiveness.

Sticking to Walls

Sticking to roofs can help keep video stabalized and drone hidden.
Steps to sticking
1.Get drone to be able to fly up.
2.Get drone to have a fan strong enough pull a vaccum.
3.Have a foam or rubber seal around fan to help pull vaccum.
4.Have button on control to activate fan when needed.
5.Have button to deactivate fan.
